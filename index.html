Privacy Policy

Introduction

Our Text Moment app is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure[cite: 1, 2].

Key Privacy Points

  • Data Transmission: For AI features, content is temporarily transmitted to API services for processing but is not collected or stored by us[cite: 2].
  • Mixed Processing: While basic scanning happens locally on your device, AI features require processing via secure external APIs[cite: 3].
  • Limited Analytics: We collect minimal, anonymous usage data solely to improve app functionality and fix technical issues[cite: 4].
  • Local Storage Only: All scanned documents are stored only on your device[cite: 5].
  • Selective Network Access: The app operates offline for basic features but requires internet connection for AI functionality[cite: 6].

App Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: Required for image scanning and QR code reading[cite: 6, 7].
  • Storage: Required to save scanned documents on your device[cite: 7].
  • Internet: Required for AI-powered features and anonymous crash reporting[cite: 7, 8].

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and not for advertising[cite: 8]. These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and not for commercial data collection[cite: 8, 9].

Data Processing

  • When using AI features, document content is temporarily transmitted to our secure API providers for processing[cite: 10].
  • We do not store or retain your document content on our servers after processing[cite: 11].
  • The transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols[cite: 12].
  • No personally identifying information is linked to your document content[cite: 12].

Analytics and Crash Reporting

  • We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics that help us improve the app[cite: 13].
  • We collect crash reports through Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix technical issues[cite: 14].
  • This data is not linked to your identity and contains no information about your document contents[cite: 15].
  • Analytics data we collect includes: app feature usage, session duration, and device information[cite: 16].
  • You can use the app's essential features without an internet connection, which prevents any analytics collection[cite: 17].

Data Storage

  • All scanned documents are stored locally in your device's private app storage[cite: 18].
  • You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time[cite: 19].
  • Other apps cannot access the documents stored by our app[cite: 20].
  • We use Firebase Firestore solely to synchronize app settings and do not store document contents online[cite: 21].

Children's Privacy

  • Our app does not collect any personal information from users of any age[cite: 22].
  • The anonymous usage data collected cannot be used to identify individual users[cite: 23].

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page[cite: 23, 24].
