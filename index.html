Privacy Policy

Last updated on March 15, 2025

Our Text Moment app is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure.

Key Privacy Points

  • Data Transmission: For AI features, content is temporarily transmitted to API services for processing but is not collected or stored by us.
  • Mixed Processing: While basic scanning happens locally on your device, AI features require processing via secure external APIs.
  • Limited Analytics: We collect minimal, anonymous usage data solely to improve app functionality and fix technical issues.
  • Local Storage Only: All scanned documents are stored only on your device.
  • Selective Network Access: The app operates offline for basic features but requires internet connection for AI functionality.

App Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: Required for image scanning and QR code reading.
  • Storage: Required to save scanned documents on your devices.
  • Internet: Required for AI-powered features and anonymous crash reporting.

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and not for commercial data collection.

Data Processing

  • When using AI features, document content is temporarily transmitted to our secure API providers for processing.
  • We do not store or retain your document content on our servers after processing.
  • The transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
  • No personally identifying information is linked to your document content.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

  • We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics that help us improve the app.
  • We collect crash reports through Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix technical issues.
  • This data is not linked to your identity and contains no information about your document contents.
  • Analytics data we collect includes: app feature usage, session duration, end device information.
  • You can use the app's essential features without an internet connection, which prevents any analytics collection.

Data Storage

  • All scanned documents are stored locally in your device's private app storage.
  • You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time.
  • Other apps cannot access the documents stored by our app.
  • We use Firebase Firestore solely to synchronize app settings and do not store document contents online.

Children's Privacy

  • Our app does not collect any personal information from users of any age.
  • The anonymous usage data collected cannot be used to identify individual users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
